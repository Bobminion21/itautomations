Features

Everything You Need for Digital Signage

📺

Real-Time WebRTC Streaming

Peer-to-peer content delivery with near-zero latency. DataChannels push content directly from dashboard to displays.

🌐

Multi-Display Support

Connect unlimited display screens. Target individual displays or broadcast to all at once from a single dashboard.

📅

Content Scheduling

Schedule playlists with start/end times and repeat options. Daily, weekdays, weekends, or weekly scheduling.

🖼️

Images, Text & Web

Push images (PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP, SVG), custom text slides, or any web URL to your displays.

🔒

Self-Hosted & Private

Runs entirely on your local network. No cloud dependency, no data sent externally, no recurring SaaS fees.

🔄

Auto-Reconnection

Displays automatically reconnect after network drops. Fallback content keeps screens running even when offline.

How SignCast Works

A lightweight Node.js signaling server connects the publisher dashboard to display clients via WebRTC.
